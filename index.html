I am a Postdoctoral Researcher at the DFG Funded Project Cognitive Rational Reconstruction (KoRaRe) at Heinrich-Heine-Universität Düsseldorf. In October 2025, I will begin my own DFG-funded research position with the project Trust and Trustworthiness and their Implications for Testimony and Epistemic Authority at Technische Universität Berlin.

Previously, I was a Postdoctoral Researcher at Humboldt Universität zu Berlin in the research training Group Philosophy Science and the Sciences. I received my PhD from Freie Universität Berlin under the supervision of Dirk Koppelberg and Barbara Vetter.
